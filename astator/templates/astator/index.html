Welcome to Astator

Here as an author? You're at the right place. Astator is a small web app built around the needs of authors and aspiring authors. It enables you to share your work with other people and get first-hand impressions, suggestions, and comments before your work gets published. On Astator, you can share your concepts (initial ideas with the overall plot and story characters), short stories, and drafts of your novels/scripts. Click on the button bellow to add your new script. Or go to your profile and make sure it stands out!
