Welcome to ASTATOR

Astator is a small, members-only, web app built around the needs of authors and aspiring authors. It enables them to share their work with other people and get first-hand impressions, suggestions, and comments before getting their work published. On Astator, authors can share their concepts (initial ideas with the overall plot and story characters), short stories, and drafts of their novels, screenplays, etc. Readers can read everything on the Astator for free.
